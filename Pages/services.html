Our Services

At Accountiva, we provide comprehensive financial solutions tailored to your business needs. Our services range from core accounting and tax compliance to financial advisory, risk management, and business growth strategies. Through both in-house expertise and trusted partnerships, we ensure your business stays compliant, financially healthy, and ready for growth.

Core Accounting & Compliance Services
1. Accounting Records & Bookkeeping:
  • Maintain accurate financial records and daily transactions to ensure clean, organized books for your business.
2. Financial Statement Preparation:
  • Prepare reliable balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow reports for informed decision-making.
3. Payroll Management:
  • Handle employee payments, tax withholdings, and compliance with labor regulations efficiently.
4. Timely Tax Compliance & Advisory
  • Ensure on-time tax filings and receive expert guidance to stay compliant with evolving tax laws.
5. Tax Strategy & Optimization:
  • Develop smart tax strategies to reduce liabilities while staying fully compliant.
6. Regulatory Compliance & Reporting:
  • Assist with meeting legal and financial reporting requirements based on your industry and jurisdiction.
Financial Advisory & Risk Management
1. Financial Consultation & Supervision:
  • Provide expert financial guidance to improve your company’s overall financial health.
2. Cash Flow Optimization:
  • Improve liquidity by managing inflows and outflows to support stable growth.
3. Budgeting & Forecasting Solutions:
  • Create accurate budgets and financial forecasts to help you plan with confidence.
4. Data Analytics & Performance Reporting
  • Use data-driven insights to track performance and support strategic decisions.
5. Company Valuation:
  • Determine the market value of your business for investment, sale, or growth purposes.
6. Internal Audit:
  • Conduct independent reviews of your operations to ensure transparency and mitigate risks.
Business Growth Solutions
1. Commercial Banking Support :
  • Assist with business loans, credit lines, and banking relationships for better financial positioning.
2. Strategic Financial Planning:
  • Develop long-term financial plans aligned with your business goals.
3. Business Performance Optimization :
  • Identify gaps and implement improvements to boost operational and financial performance.
4. Risk Management & Mitigation
  • Detect potential risks early and develop strategies to reduce impact.
5. Conflict Resolution & Claim Settlement :
  • Support dispute handling and settlement processes with financial clarity.
6. Expansion Support :
  • Help you scale your business through financial planning, funding, and strategy.
Services in Partnership
1. Forensic Accounting :
  • Investigate financial discrepancies and support legal processes with accurate reports .
2. Due Diligence & Financial Audits :
  • Ensure accuracy and transparency in financial records during mergers, acquisitions, or investments.
3. Feasibility Studies :
  • Analyze project viability to guide sound investment and development decisions .
